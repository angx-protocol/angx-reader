# angx-reader

*A lens over angeliaX.*

---

angeliaX records what is — work built, methods learned, failures encountered, surplus freely given. At sufficient density the connections between those records become impossible to find by reading alone. A failure logged in one place and its solution logged in another may never meet.

angx-reader reads the logs a steward already holds and surfaces the ones that relate — a failure near a learning signal, a scarcity near a surplus, a method near a problem elsewhere in the partner chain. It writes nothing. It has no authority over the protocol. It surfaces matches. The steward decides what to do with them.

---

## The boundary

angeliaX does not record what is needed, requested, or wanted. That line is deliberate and angx-reader does not cross it.

The reader reads what is already logged in ANGX feeds and infers from there. A health node logging "test kits out of stock, three suspected cases unconfirmed" has recorded its week honestly. The reader reads the shape of a shortage in that signal and looks for a nearby surplus or a relevant learning signal already in the partner chain. The inference belongs to the reader. The protocol stays untouched.

---

## What it finds

Each signal becomes a point in the vector space — close to other signals about the same thing, far from the rest, regardless of language.

A failure signal near a learning signal — the same problem already solved somewhere else in the record. A shortage near a surplus — scoped to what is actually reachable. A method near a problem — documented expertise near a failure signal, logged by someone who has never heard of the person who needs it.

None of these matches move anything. Each is a pointer to two places in the existing record. The steward reads both and decides.
